Publication

Each submitted paper will be peered reviewed and only well-researched and well written papers will be accepted for presentation. All presented papers will be published in conference proceeding with ISBN Number by a reputed publisher. Selected peer reviewed papers will be considered for publication in UGC/ICI (without publication charges) and SCOPUS/SJR/WOS/SCIE Indexed journals (with publication charges).

At least one author of each accepted paper is required to register and present their paper at the conference; otherwise paper will not be included in the proceedings.
